### BOB SIMS
**Python/tkinter · WORM State**

Sovereign NPC life simulation. 12 agents, 6 needs, 6 rooms. NPCs pathfind to restore their lowest need. Every state change is WORM sealed.

### BOB PARALLIVE
**Python · Threading · WORM**

Concurrent multi-agent life. Each NPC runs its own thread. Shared world state with per-agent locks. Agents seek each other to restore social need. Up to 12 simultaneous.
